Howard Wang's China Grill made it's Grand Opening in Dallas, Texas in 2005. Since it's opening, locals have been filling up the restaurant on a daily basis for lunch and dinner. The unique, contemporary atmosphere in combination with fine Chinese dining keeps people coming back.

HW's China Grill has had overwhelming success and extremely high rate of return visitors.

The ultra contemporary atmosphere brings customers a feeling of relaxation and comfort while dining with family and friends. The food speaks for itself and is made only with the finest ingredients.
